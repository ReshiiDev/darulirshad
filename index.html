Our Mission & Vision

Guided by Islamic principles, we strive to develop scholars who will lead the Muslim Ummah with knowledge and wisdom

Our Mission

To develop an Islamic educational framework in order to prepare religious scholars ('Ulama) who acquire, practice and propagate Islam, being alert of their ultimate responsibilities towards Almighty Allah and towards themselves, family, institution, society, surroundings and private and public resources and to make them capable of competing the shifting trends of modern education.

Our Vision

DIA aims to be the bastion of expertise in Islamic education that they can restore the dynamic role and educational superiority of past Muslim society in all intellectual programs and that seeks to rekindle the spirit of scholarship in Islamic sciences in the era of ever-changing society due to the innovative growth of modern sciences.

Philosophy

DIA is founded upon the educational philosophy of Islam which in turn is inspired by the principles of hierarchy and unity of knowledge in its intents, contents and methods. Therefore, our philosophy is based on recognizing Allah, the Almighty as the fountain-head of knowledge (al 'allam); acknowledging Him as the ultimate end of every human endeavor.

Islamic Academic Programs

Comprehensive Islamic education combining traditional scholarship with contemporary relevance

Quranic Studies

Comprehensive study of Holy Quran including Tafseer, Qira'at, and Uloom al-Quran to develop deep understanding of divine guidance.

Hadith Sciences

In-depth study of Prophetic traditions including Uloom al-Hadith, authentication methods, and application in contemporary context.

Islamic Jurisprudence

Classical and contemporary Fiqh studies, Usool al-Fiqh, and comparative jurisprudence to prepare competent Islamic jurists.

Islamic Theology

Aqeedah, Kalam, and Islamic philosophy programs fostering strong theological foundation and intellectual discourse.

Islamic History & Civilization

Study of Islamic heritage, civilization, and contributions to human knowledge and cultural development.

Da'wah & Community Service

Training in Islamic propagation, interfaith dialogue, and community leadership for effective social engagement.
